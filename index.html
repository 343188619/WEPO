About Us

世界环境保护组织简介

Introduction to WEPO

世界环境保护组织(WEPO)成立于2010年，是一个非营利性国际环保组织，总部位于中国重庆。我们致力于通过科学研究、政策倡导和公众教育来推动全球环境保护事业。

World Environmental Protection Organization (WEPO), founded in 2010, is a non-profit international environmental organization headquartered in Chongqing, China. We are committed to promoting global environmental protection through scientific research, policy advocacy, and public education.

我们的工作涵盖气候变化、生物多样性保护、水资源管理、污染防治等多个领域。通过与政府、企业、学术机构和社区的合作，我们致力于寻找切实可行的解决方案，以应对当今世界面临的最紧迫的环境挑战。

Our work covers various fields including climate change, biodiversity conservation, water resource management, and pollution prevention. Through collaboration with governments, businesses, academic institutions, and communities, we strive to find practical solutions to address the most pressing environmental challenges facing our world today.

WEPO在全球拥有超过500名专业工作人员和数千名志愿者，在30多个国家开展环境保护项目。我们的愿景是创造一个人类与自然和谐共处的可持续未来。

WEPO has over 500 professional staff and thousands of volunteers worldwide, implementing environmental protection projects in more than 30 countries. Our vision is to create a sustainable future where humans and nature coexist in harmony.

使命与愿景

Mission & Vision

我们的使命

Our Mission

通过科学研究、政策倡导和公众教育，推动全球环境保护事业，应对气候变化，保护生物多样性，促进可持续发展。

To promote global environmental protection through scientific research, policy advocacy, and public education, addressing climate change, protecting biodiversity, and fostering sustainable development.

我们的愿景

Our Vision

创造一个人类与自然和谐共处的世界，在这个世界里，经济发展与环境保护相互促进，所有生命都能繁荣昌盛。

To create a world where humans and nature coexist in harmony, where economic development and environmental protection mutually reinforce each other, and where all life can thrive.

核心价值观

Core Values

科学严谨、合作共赢、公正透明、勇于创新。我们相信环境保护需要全球合作，需要基于科学的解决方案，需要每个人的参与。

Scientific rigor, collaborative win-win, fairness and transparency, and innovation. We believe that environmental protection requires global cooperation, science-based solutions, and everyone's participation.

环保项目

Environmental Programs

气候变化应对

Climate Change Response

通过减排项目、碳汇林建设和气候适应计划，帮助社区应对气候变化的影响。

Helping communities address the impacts of climate change through emission reduction projects, carbon sink forests, and climate adaptation plans.

生物多样性保护

Biodiversity Conservation

建立自然保护区，保护濒危物种，恢复生态系统，维护地球生命网络。

Establishing nature reserves, protecting endangered species, restoring ecosystems, and maintaining Earth's web of life.

水资源保护

Water Resource Protection

保护淡水资源，改善水质，推广节水技术，确保人人享有清洁饮用水。

Protecting freshwater resources, improving water quality, promoting water-saving technologies, and ensuring clean drinking water for all.

可持续城市

Sustainable Cities

推动绿色建筑、公共交通、废物管理和城市绿化，建设宜居可持续的城市环境。

Promoting green buildings, public transportation, waste management, and urban greening to build livable and sustainable urban environments.

环境教育

Environmental Education

通过学校课程、公众活动和媒体宣传，提高全社会的环境意识和环保技能。

Enhancing environmental awareness and skills throughout society through school curricula, public activities, and media campaigns.

政策倡导

Policy Advocacy

基于科学研究，为政府和企业提供政策建议，推动环境友好型政策和法规的制定与实施。

Providing policy recommendations to governments and businesses based on scientific research to promote the development and implementation of environmentally friendly policies and regulations.

合作伙伴

Partners

WEPO与全球各地的政府机构、非政府组织、企业和学术机构建立了广泛的合作伙伴关系，共同推动环境保护事业。

WEPO has established extensive partnerships with government agencies, NGOs, businesses, and academic institutions around the world to jointly promote environmental protection.
