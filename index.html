About Us

Building a sustainable future where humans and nature coexist.

Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Chongqing, China, the Weco Ecology Protection Organization (WEPO) is a non-profit international NGO standing at the forefront of the global environmental crisis.

From mitigating climate change to reversing biodiversity loss, we are dedicated to finding and implementing practical, science-based solutions.

We actively collaborate with governmental bodies, indigenous communities, and multinational enterprises to drive systemic, long-lasting change.

Today, our dedicated professional teams and passionate volunteers span across more than 30 countries.
