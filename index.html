About Us

Building a sustainable future where humans and nature coexist.

Founded in 2010 and headquartered in Chongqing, China, WEPO is a non-profit international organization at the forefront of environmental protection.

From climate change to biodiversity loss, we seek practical solutions through science, policy, and education.
