About Us

Building a sustainable future where humans and nature coexist.

Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Chongqing, China, the Weco Ecology Protection Organization (WEPO) is a non-profit international NGO standing at the forefront of the global environmental crisis.

From mitigating climate change to reversing biodiversity loss, we are dedicated to finding and implementing practical, science-based solutions. Through rigorous policy advocacy, public education, and grassroots initiatives, we strive to bridge the gap between human development and ecological preservation.

We actively collaborate with governmental bodies, indigenous communities, and multinational enterprises to drive systemic, long-lasting change. By deploying advanced ecological monitoring networks and championing green technologies, we empower millions of individuals to become proactive stewards of the Earth.

Today, our dedicated professional teams and passionate volunteers span across more than 30 countries. Together, we are working tirelessly to protect vulnerable habitats, reshape sustainable policies, and inspire the next generation to safeguard our common home.

Core Values

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Driving global environmental protection through rigorous scientific research, proactive policy advocacy, and widespread public education to mitigate climate impact.

Our Vision

Creating a thriving, resilient world where humanity and nature coexist in perfect harmony, ensuring abundant resources for all future generations.

Our Values

Scientific rigor, borderless collaboration, unwavering transparency, and relentless innovation in tackling today's toughest ecological challenges.

Focus Areas

Programs

Climate Action

Helping vulnerable communities adapt to climate change, advancing the global transition to renewable energy, and driving the ultimate goal of carbon neutrality.

Biodiversity

Protecting endangered wildlife, combating illegal poaching, and meticulously restoring degraded forests, wetlands, and marine ecosystems.

Sustainable Cities

Promoting green building standards, circular economy practices, and sustainable urban mobility to create zero-carbon, livable environments.
