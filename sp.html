This powerful PSA presents nature's first-person perspective, warning humanity: "Nature doesn't need people. People need nature." Voiced by celebrities including Jiang Wenli and Jiang Wen.

迁徙

Migration

这部获奖环保题材动画短片讲述了两只北极熊因全球变暖被迫迁徙的故事，它们将在旅途中遇到棕熊并尝试共同生活。法国学生作品，配乐精良。

This award-winning animated short tells the story of two polar bears forced to migrate due to global warming, encountering brown bears along their journey. A French student production with excellent soundtrack.
