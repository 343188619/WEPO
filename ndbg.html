全球气候变化影响评估报告2025

Global Climate Change Impact Assessment Report 2025

气候变化 Climate Change 2025年6月 June 2025 48页

报告摘要

Report Summary

本报告基于全球气候观测数据和模型模拟结果，全面评估了气候变化对生态系统、农业、水资源、人类健康和经济社会发展的影响。报告显示，全球平均气温较工业化前水平已上升1.2°C，极端天气事件频率和强度显著增加。海洋酸化加剧，生物多样性丧失速度加快，粮食安全面临严峻挑战。报告提出了基于自然的解决方案和适应性管理策略，为各国制定气候政策提供科学依据。

Based on global climate observation data and model simulation results, this report comprehensively assesses the impacts of climate change on ecosystems, agriculture, water resources, human health, and economic and social development. The report shows that the global average temperature has risen by 1.2°C compared to pre-industrial levels, with significant increases in the frequency and intensity of extreme weather events. Ocean acidification is intensifying, biodiversity loss is accelerating, and food security faces severe challenges. The report proposes nature-based solutions and adaptive management strategies, providing a scientific basis for countries to formulate climate policies.

主要发现

Key Findings

  • 全球平均气温上升速度超出预期，2024年成为有记录以来最热年份
  • 极端高温事件频率增加5倍，极端降水事件增加30%
  • 北极海冰范围达到历史最低水平，冰川退缩速度加快
  • 海洋热含量创历史新高，海洋酸化威胁海洋生态系统
  • 全球粮食产量预计到2050年将下降5-15%
  • The rate of global average temperature rise exceeds expectations, with 2024 becoming the hottest year on record
  • Frequency of extreme heat events increased fivefold, extreme precipitation events increased by 30%
  • Arctic sea ice extent reached historic lows, glacier retreat accelerated
  • Ocean heat content reached record highs, ocean acidification threatens marine ecosystems
  • Global food production is projected to decline by 5-15% by 2050

政策建议

Policy Recommendations

  • 加速能源转型，2030年前将可再生能源占比提高至50%
  • 加强生态系统保护和恢复，提升自然碳汇能力
  • 建立气候适应型农业体系，保障粮食安全
  • 完善极端天气预警系统，增强社区抗灾能力
  • 加强国际合作，建立全球气候治理新机制
  • Accelerate energy transition, increase renewable energy share to 50% by 2030
  • Strengthen ecosystem protection and restoration to enhance natural carbon sinks
  • Establish climate-resilient agricultural systems to ensure food security
  • Improve extreme weather early warning systems and enhance community disaster resilience
  • Strengthen international cooperation to establish new mechanisms for global climate governance
