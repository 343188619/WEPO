2024年度报告

2024 Annual Report

世界环境保护组织2024年度报告展示了我们在全球环境保护领域取得的重大成就、关键数据和未来规划。

The World Environmental Protection Organization's 2024 Annual Report showcases our major achievements, key data, and future plans in global environmental protection.

年度回顾

Year in Review

2024 Q1

全球气候倡议启动

Global Climate Initiative Launch

我们在全球启动了10个气候适应项目，帮助脆弱社区应对气候变化影响。

We launched 10 climate adaptation projects worldwide to help vulnerable communities cope with climate change impacts.

10个国家 10 Countries
50万受益人 500K Beneficiaries
2024 Q2

海洋保护行动

Ocean Protection Campaign

清理了超过100吨海洋塑料垃圾，建立了5个海洋保护区。

Cleaned over 100 tons of marine plastic waste and established 5 marine protected areas.

100吨垃圾 100 Tons Waste
5个保护区 5 Protected Areas
2024 Q3

森林恢复计划

Forest Restoration Program

在亚马逊、刚果和东南亚种植了超过100万棵树，恢复了5000公顷森林。

Planted over 1 million trees in the Amazon, Congo, and Southeast Asia, restoring 5,000 hectares of forest.

100万棵树 1M Trees
5000公顷 5,000 Hectares
2024 Q4

可再生能源项目

Renewable Energy Projects

在非洲和亚洲偏远地区安装了500套太阳能系统，减少了2000吨碳排放。

Installed 500 solar systems in remote areas of Africa and Asia, reducing 2,000 tons of carbon emissions.

500套系统 500 Systems
2000吨减排 2K Tons Reduced

Ocean Cleanup Campaign

Cleaned 100 tons of marine plastic waste, established 5 marine protected areas to protect endangered marine species.

Forest Restoration

百万树木计划

Million Trees Program

Planted 1 million trees in three major rainforest regions, restoring 5,000 hectares of forest ecosystems.

环境教育
Environmental Education

全球环境教育

Global Environmental Education

在30个国家开展环境教育项目，培训了10000名教师，影响了50万学生。

Conducted environmental education programs in 30 countries, trained 10,000 teachers, and impacted 500,000 students.

30个国家 30 Countries
1万教师 10K Teachers
政策倡导
Policy Advocacy

环保政策影响

Environmental Policy Impact

促成15个国家采纳更严格的环境保护政策，推动了全球塑料污染条约谈判。

Facilitated 15 countries to adopt stricter environmental policies and promoted global plastic pollution treaty negotiations.

15个国家 15 Countries
全球条约 Global Treaty
