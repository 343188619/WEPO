组织章程

Constitution

世界环境保护组织的根本制度文件，规定了组织的宗旨、结构、运作方式和成员权利义务

The fundamental document of WEPO, defining the organization's purpose, structure, operations, and members' rights and obligations

世界环境保护组织章程

Constitution of World Environmental Protection Organization

第一章 总则

Chapter 1 General Provisions

1

名称与性质

Name and Nature

本组织名称为"世界环境保护组织"（World Environmental Protection Organization，简称WEPO），是一个非政府、非营利性的国际环保组织。

The organization shall be named "World Environmental Protection Organization" (abbreviated as WEPO), a non-governmental, non-profit international environmental organization.

2

宗旨

Purpose

WEPO的宗旨是通过科学研究、政策倡导和公众教育，推动全球环境保护事业，应对气候变化，保护生物多样性，促进可持续发展。

The purpose of WEPO is to promote global environmental protection through scientific research, policy advocacy, and public education, addressing climate change, protecting biodiversity, and fostering sustainable development.

3

总部

Headquarters

WEPO总部设在中国重庆市，可在全球各地设立分支机构或代表处。

The headquarters of WEPO is located in Chongqing, China, and may establish branches or representative offices worldwide.

第二章 会员

Chapter 2 Membership

4

会员资格

Membership Eligibility

WEPO会员分为个人会员和团体会员：

WEPO membership consists of individual members and institutional members:

  • 个人会员：年满18周岁，认同本组织宗旨，愿意为环境保护事业贡献力量的自然人
  • Individual members: Natural persons aged 18 or above who endorse the organization's purpose and are willing to contribute to environmental protection
  • 团体会员：依法成立的法人或其他组织，认同本组织宗旨，愿意参与环境保护活动
  • Institutional members: Legally established legal entities or other organizations that endorse the organization's purpose and are willing to participate in environmental protection activities
5

会员权利

Membership Rights

WEPO会员享有以下权利：

WEPO members enjoy the following rights:

  • 参与本组织的各项活动
  • Participate in all activities of the organization
  • 获得本组织提供的服务和信息
  • Receive services and information provided by the organization
  • 对本组织工作提出建议和意见
  • Provide suggestions and opinions on the organization's work
  • 选举和被选举为理事会成员
  • Elect and be elected as members of the Council
  • 自愿退会的权利
  • The right to voluntarily withdraw from membership
6

会员义务

Membership Obligations

WEPO会员应履行以下义务：

WEPO members shall fulfill the following obligations:

  • 遵守本组织章程和各项规章制度
  • Comply with the organization's constitution and regulations
  • 维护本组织的声誉和利益
  • Safeguard the reputation and interests of the organization
  • 积极参与本组织的活动
  • Actively participate in the organization's activities
  • 按时缴纳会费
  • Pay membership fees on time
  • 履行理事会规定的其他义务
  • Fulfill other obligations stipulated by the Council

第三章 组织机构

Chapter 3 Organizational Structure

7

会员大会

General Assembly

会员大会是WEPO的最高权力机构，由全体会员组成，每年召开一次会议。会员大会的职责包括：

The General Assembly is the supreme authority of WEPO, composed of all members, and convenes once a year. The responsibilities of the General Assembly include:

  • 制定和修改章程
  • Formulating and amending the constitution
  • 选举和罢免理事会成员
  • Electing and dismissing Council members
  • 审议理事会工作报告和财务报告
  • Reviewing the Council's work reports and financial reports
  • 决定本组织的重大事项
  • Deciding on major matters of the organization
8

理事会

Council

理事会是WEPO的执行机构，由会员大会选举产生，任期三年，可连选连任。理事会设主席一人，副主席若干人，秘书长一人。理事会的主要职责包括：

The Council is the executive body of WEPO, elected by the General Assembly for a term of three years and eligible for re-election. The Council consists of one Chairperson, several Vice-Chairpersons, and one Secretary-General. The main responsibilities of the Council include:

  • 执行会员大会的决议
  • Implementing resolutions of the General Assembly
  • 制定本组织的工作计划和预算
  • Formulating the organization's work plans and budgets
  • 管理本组织的日常事务
  • Managing the organization's daily affairs
  • 决定分支机构的设立和撤销
  • Deciding on the establishment and dissolution of branches
  • 任命和罢免秘书长
  • Appointing and dismissing the Secretary-General
9

秘书处

Secretariat

秘书处是WEPO的常设办事机构，由秘书长领导，负责处理本组织的日常事务。秘书处的主要职责包括：

The Secretariat is the permanent administrative body of WEPO, led by the Secretary-General, responsible for handling the organization's daily affairs. The main responsibilities of the Secretariat include:

  • 执行理事会的决议
  • Implementing resolutions of the Council
  • 协调各部门和各分支机构的工作
  • Coordinating the work of various departments and branches
  • 管理本组织的财务和资产
  • Managing the organization's finances and assets
  • 组织各类活动和项目
  • Organizing various activities and projects
  • 处理会员事务
  • Handling membership affairs
10

专业委员会

Special Committees

WEPO可根据工作需要设立专业委员会，如科学委员会、政策委员会、教育委员会等。各专业委员会由相关领域的专家组成，负责提供专业咨询和建议。

WEPO may establish Special Committees as needed, such as the Science Committee, Policy Committee, Education Committee, etc. Each Special Committee consists of experts in relevant fields, responsible for providing professional advice and recommendations.

第四章 财务管理

Chapter 4 Financial Management

11

资金来源

Funding Sources

WEPO的资金来源包括：

WEPO's funding sources include:

  • 会员会费
  • Membership fees
  • 社会捐赠
  • Social donations
  • 政府资助
  • Government grants
  • 项目收入
  • Project income
  • 其他合法收入
  • Other lawful income
12

资金使用

Use of Funds

WEPO的资金必须用于本章程规定的业务范围和事业发展，不得在会员中分配。本组织的资产管理必须执行国家规定的财务管理制度，接受会员大会和财政部门的监督。

WEPO's funds must be used for the business scope and development as stipulated in this constitution and shall not be distributed among members. The organization's asset management must comply with the financial management system stipulated by the state and be subject to supervision by the General Assembly and financial departments.

13

财务监督

Financial Supervision

WEPO设立监事会，负责监督本组织的财务活动。监事会由会员大会选举产生，任期三年。监事会的职责包括：

WEPO establishes a Supervisory Board responsible for supervising the organization's financial activities. The Supervisory Board is elected by the General Assembly for a term of three years. The responsibilities of the Supervisory Board include:

  • 监督理事会和秘书处的财务活动
  • Supervising the financial activities of the Council and Secretariat
  • 审查本组织的财务报告
  • Reviewing the organization's financial reports
  • 向会员大会报告监督情况
  • Reporting supervision results to the General Assembly
  • 提出改进财务管理的建议
  • Providing recommendations for improving financial management

第五章 章程修改与终止

Chapter 5 Amendments and Termination

14

章程修改

Amendments

本章程的修改须经会员大会三分之二以上会员表决通过，并报相关部门备案后生效。

Amendments to this constitution must be approved by more than two-thirds of the members at the General Assembly and shall take effect after being filed with relevant authorities.

15

组织终止

Termination

WEPO完成宗旨或自行解散或由于分立、合并等原因需要注销的，由理事会提出终止动议，经会员大会表决通过，并报相关部门审查同意后，按国家有关规定处理剩余财产。

If WEPO completes its purpose, voluntarily dissolves, or needs to be terminated due to division, merger, or other reasons, the Council shall propose a termination motion, which shall be approved by the General Assembly and reviewed and agreed upon by relevant authorities. The remaining assets shall be handled according to relevant national regulations.

第六章 附则

Chapter 6 Supplementary Provisions

16

生效日期

Effective Date

本章程自2021年5月22日起生效。

This constitution shall take effect from May 22, 2021.

17

解释权

Interpretation

本章程的解释权属于WEPO理事会。

The interpretation of this constitution belongs to the WEPO Council.

世界环境保护组织(WEPO)是一个致力于全球环境保护的非营利性国际组织，通过科学研究、政策倡导和公众教育推动可持续发展。

World Environmental Protection Organization (WEPO) is a non-profit international organization dedicated to global environmental protection, promoting sustainable development through scientific research, policy advocacy, and public education.
