Organization Charter

Understanding our organizational structure, mission, and operational principles.

Download Full PDF ↓

Chapter 1: General Provisions

01

Name

The official name of the organization is "World Environmental Protection Organization" (WEPO).

02

Nature

WEPO is a non-profit, non-governmental international environmental organization dedicated to global environmental protection and sustainable development.

03

Mission

The mission is to promote global environmental awareness, advance eco-friendly technologies, coordinate international actions, and protect Earth's ecosystems.

Chapter 2: Membership

04

Eligibility

Individuals or organizations who agree with our mission and are willing to participate may apply:

  • Individual Member: Natural persons aged 18 or above with full civil capacity.
  • Organization Member: Legally established enterprises, institutions, and social groups.

WEPO is dedicated to global environmental protection.
